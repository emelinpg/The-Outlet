Q: Is there other locations?

We are hoping to expand in the future and open more stores but for now we only have the location in Azusa.

Q: How do you get your merchandise

The Outlet buys and sells "Retail Salvage Merchandise". This includes freight damage, returns, floor models, closeouts and whatever great deals come along. Most items are new but there are reconditioned/used as well. Please inspect items carefully as merchandise is sold in "AS IS" condition. Packages may be inspected at the front desk.

Q: What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, check or credit/debit card with valid ID.

Q: What days are you open?

We are open 7 days a week. Monday-Saturday 9:00am-7:00pm and Sunday 10:00am-5:00pm. The only holidays we are closed are Easter, Fourth of July, Thanksgiving and Christmas.
